# Moonmarket Gloam Arcade

Beneath suspended lantern vines, merchants trade in whispers, contracts, and glittering rumors.

- Best visited after second bell.
- Rumor brokers cluster near the old fountain.
- The Sable Ledger keeps records in a hidden back room.
